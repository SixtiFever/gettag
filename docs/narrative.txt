Mission Statement
To reimagine global video debate through a frictionless creation process

Problem
Traditional video debating is limited by real time coordination. Whether on panels, podcasts or a YouTube show, logistics and scheduling limit participation and subsequently the quantity and quality of debates.

Solution
Tag is a debate creation app that lets users build, join and share asynchronous video debates – removing real-time constraints that limit traditional video debating, and providing cross-platform posting to existing audiences.

Why it works
Polarisation is increasing demand for social media content. Social media content is shifting towards reel video format. Thus, Tag helps creators create debate content audiences need in a format they already love.

How it works
Record - Make your point in a 20 second Take
Tag - Invite another user to respond to your Take
Grow - Watch the debate grow as more people get involved and have their say

Key features
Tagging system - Invite anyone into a debate to have their say, at a time convenient for them.
Bite-sized takes - 20 second takes keep points sharp and concise
Cross platform posting - Share individual takes or full debate chains to any platform


Elevator pitch
Increasing polarisation has driven demand for conversational social media content, particularly video debating. However, current video debates require real-time participation. This makes it tricky to find a time convenient for everyone — especially with participants across time zones. That's where Tag comes in. Tag is a mobile app where users build video debates asynchronously and publish them to their existing audiences across platforms. Debates don't depend on logistics, can grow globally, and contribution is effortless — all you need is your phone and a thought.

Overview

What is Tag?

Tag

Tag is a content creation app for video debate. It lets creators record, build, and share Debate Chains — a new format of video debate built from 30-second takes.

Debate Chains

Debate Chains are a new type of social video content created in Tag. They turn debates into short, structured conversations built from 30-second video responses.

How it works

How it works

Record a 30-second take and tag someone to respond. Each response extends the chain, building a full debate one take at a time.

  1. Record

    Record a 30-second take and make your point

  2. Tag

    Tag someone to respond — their take gets appended to yours

  3. Grow

    They tag someone else — each response extends the chain

Share the full debate chain or individual takes directly to your socials.

Debate Chains

What is a Debate Chain?

Debate Chains are a new type of social video content created in Tag. They turn debates into short, structured conversations built from 30-second video responses.

  • Concise takes

    Make points in 30-second takes — snappy, quick-fire debates that reward concise point-making over rambling.

  • Simple structure

    A tagging system creates a linear, turn-based debate structure that's easy to follow for both participants and viewers.

For creators

Built for creators

Create new types of content, reach new audiences, and debate on your own schedule.

  • New types of content

    Turn opinions, disagreements, and discussions into engaging video debates your audience can follow.

  • Reach new audiences

    Each participant can share the debate chain with their own audience, creating organic discovery across communities.

  • Create without coordination

    Asynchronous tagging makes it easy to debate globally across time zones. Record your point, tag someone to respond. Done.

What is Tag?

Tag is built on a simple principle: humans create better together.

Tag brings this to life through Video Chains — a new format where friends co-create clip by clip into a single finished video.

From debating to storytelling, improv to roasting, Tag is the only place where a group creates something together, from nothing, into one piece of content.

What are Video Chains

Multiple voices. One video.

A Video Chain is a sequence of short clips, each contributed by a different person, connected into one viewable narrative. Structured, easy to follow, and ready to share.

Creating Video Chains in Tag

Co-creating with friends is easy. All you need is a phone, 30 seconds, and something to say.

  1. 1 Step 1

    Record your take in 30 seconds and tag a friend to respond

  2. 2 Step 2

    They record their response and tag you back or someone else in the group

  3. 3 Step 3

    Each take extends the chain — one voice at a time, until the video is complete

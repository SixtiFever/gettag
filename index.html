What is Tag?

Tag is an app for creating and sharing Video Chains — The video format social media has been missing.

Open, multi-voice video conversations don't currently exist as a social media content format. Despite epitomising everything social media was created for: Communication, collaboration, sharing.

Tag changes that with Video Chains. A new type of video content that's built socially and easily shared.

What are Video Chains

Video content that's collaboratively built. Take by take.

A video chain is a sequence of short-form video clips, each contributed asynchronously by a user, connected to be a single viewable video. This means each video chain can create global interactions that are structured, easy to follow and perfect for social media content.

Debate. React. Tell stories. Whatever the conversation, build it together. Build it in a Video Chain.
